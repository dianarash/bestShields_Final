​I`m a craftman who makes shields of different types and epochs. My ability to use tools, drawing and love of history combined and grew from an ordinary hobby into a job. 

I`m currently based in Lviv, but this doesn`t prevent me from sending shields around the world. ​I make shields to order and work with tasks of varying difficulty because I love experiments.

Hobby

​I 'm a member of the ​Club of Historical Reconstruction "Normans". Together with other participants, I recreate the knightly battles, dances, culinary traditions and travel. It was in this club that I was inspired by the atmosphere and started making shields myself. In our free time we take part in various medieval festivals and are the organizers of our own festival "Bastion" in Kalush.
